## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

### English (en-US)
```
KF Product Deal is a B2B wholesale marketplace connecting shops and kiosks with wholesalers. Browse products, join group deals, place orders, and track shipments—all in one app.

FOR SHOPS & BUYERS
• Browse product catalog with categories and search
• Join group buying deals and track progress
• Add to cart, checkout with multiple payment options
• Order history with status timeline
• Shipment tracking with carrier links
• Multi-language support (English, German, Russian, Arabic, and more)
• EUR and USD price display

FOR WHOLESALERS
• Manage your product catalog and variants
• Create and manage deals with target quantities
• Process orders and update status
• Track revenue from product and deal orders
• Send payment instructions to customers

FEATURES
• Secure login with JWT authentication
• Role-based access (buyer, wholesaler, admin)
• Push notifications for orders, deal updates, and shipments
• Stories for promotions (WhatsApp-style)
• Wholesaler profiles and product browsing
• Variant selection (size, color, etc.)
• Shipping address management

Perfect for kiosks, convenience stores, and retail shops looking to source products from wholesalers at competitive prices. Join deals to unlock better rates through group buying.
```
